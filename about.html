About the Goldbach Orbit Method

This project presents the Goldbach Orbit Method, a predictive algorithm to find prime pairs (p, q) such that p + q = E, where E is a very large even number. The method is inspired by the structure of orbital shells and guided by the square root of E, exploring central, orbital, and peripheral zones to identify solutions.

Author

Bouchaib Bouchaib is an independent researcher in mathematics based in Nantes, France. He has developed several innovative methods around number theory, biprime factorization, and Goldbach decompositions. This site is part of his public research efforts.

About the Site

The website allows anyone to enter a very large even number and instantly test the Goldbach Orbit Method online. It uses modern JavaScript with BigInt and root-based estimations to extend predictions beyond 10¹⁹.
